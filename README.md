## ✍️ Motivation for This Document

I wrote this document to help you understand how I work, what I care about, and how we can collaborate effectively. My goal is to create an environment where **trust, transparency, growth, and fellowship** are not just values but daily practices.

I want you to feel empowered to ask questions, challenge ideas, and bring your full self to work.

---

## 🧑‍💻 My Role

I’m responsible for building and scaling high-performing, cross-functional engineering teams. My success is measured by:

- Team health and engagement  
- Delivery of value-focused outcomes  
- Growth of individuals within the team  

I help you improve by providing clear expectations, continuous feedback, development opportunities, and the psychological safety to take smart risks.

---

## 🧭 What I Value Most

- **Trust and Transparency** — I’d rather hear bad news early than have issues hidden. Problems are solvable when surfaced.  
- **Ownership** — I expect you to own your work, and I will support you. Ask for help when needed — it’s a strength, not a weakness.  
- **Continuous Learning** — Mistakes are part of growth. Learn, adapt, and move forward.  
- **Outcome over Output** — Solving the right problems matters more than ticking tasks off a list.  
- **Kindness and Respect** — Strong opinions are welcome, but respect is non-negotiable.

### How You Can Help Me

- Communicate openly  
- Propose solutions alongside problems  
- Challenge my thinking when you disagree — it makes us all better

### My Weaknesses

I get excited about big ideas and can sometimes move fast. If things feel rushed or unclear, pull me aside — I’ll appreciate it.

---

## 📋 My Expectations

- **Communication** — Over-communicate if you’re unsure. Early signals beat late surprises.  
- **Availability** — Generally available 9:00–17:00 CET. Outside of that, emergencies only.  
- **Meetings** — Join 1–2 minutes early when possible. Cameras on if bandwidth allows.  
- **Mistakes** — Mistakes are okay; hiding them isn’t. Let’s address and learn quickly.  
- **Definition of Done** — Clear ownership, tested, documented if necessary, and adds value.

---

## 💬 1:1s

- **Cadence** — Biweekly, plus ad-hoc when you prefer  
- **Who Leads** — You do. It’s your time.  
- **Focus** — Your growth, well-being, feedback (both ways), and anything on your mind  
- **Expectations** — Bring topics if you can, but no pressure. Some of the best conversations are unscripted.

---

## 🎭 Personality Quirks

- I value humor and lightness — a small joke can improve a tough day.  
- I may ask many questions out of curiosity, not doubt.  
- I deeply appreciate proactive behavior — stepping up earns my respect.  
- I can get intensely focused; if I seem distant, a quick ping brings me back.
